# Obsidian Flashcards Sample

## Format 1: Q:/A: Style

Q: What is the Pythagorean theorem?
A: a² + b² = c² - In a right triangle, the square of the hypotenuse equals the sum of squares of the other two sides

Q: Who painted the Mona Lisa?
A: Leonardo da Vinci painted it in the early 16th century

Q: What is the speed of light?
A: Approximately 299,792,458 meters per second in vacuum

## Format 2: Question:/Answer: Style

Question: What is machine learning?
Answer: A subset of AI where systems learn from data without explicit programming

Question: Define recursion
Answer: A programming technique where a function calls itself to solve a problem

## Format 3: Heading Style

## What is blockchain?
A distributed ledger technology that maintains a secure and decentralized record of transactions

## Explain quantum computing
Computing that uses quantum-mechanical phenomena like superposition and entanglement to perform operations on data

## What is the greenhouse effect?
Process where atmospheric gases trap heat from the sun, warming Earth's surface
